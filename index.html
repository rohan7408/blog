We all know about Ronaldo Nazario's famous triangle Hairstyle in 2002
Worldcup. Do you know why he chose that hairstyle: He says, "I had a
leg injury and everybody kept asking about it which made me irritated.
I decided to cut my hair and leave the small thing there. When I came
to training everybody saw and started talking about my bad hair. They
forgot my injury. It helped me become calm and relaxed and focused on
my training. I am not proud of that hairstyle but it was good to change subject "
